Fragrance Desing

I designed a new fragrance for CHER Beauty’s Onyx line, projected for 2026. Conceived for a more mature and sophisticated audience, Onyx contrasts with Dieciocho, the brand’s youthful line, through a refined aesthetic, deeper tones, and a sensorial language focused on elegance and intensity.

The project was guided by a brand-provided moodboard that explored amber hues, tortoiseshell textures, and a nocturnal atmosphere.

Serving as a visual reference to define materiality, color palette, and the overall sensorial direction of the fragrance in line with current luxury trends.

This new fragrance stands out through its tortoiseshell cap, a distinctive element that differentiates it within the collection while preserving the bottle’s iconic silhouette. The design reinforces its individuality without breaking the visual coherence of the Onyx line, ensuring the fragrance remains clearly part of the same family.
