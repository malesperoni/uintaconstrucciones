Packaging Seville Fair

This project consists of a tasting set packaging for churros, designed in a fan-shaped format for the Seville Fair. The design works both as a cultural reference and as an ergonomic solution, allowing users to walk comfortably while holding the package with one hand.

Each churro flavor is placed in a dedicated compartment, preventing mixing and clearly guiding the tasting experience through an intuitive and organized layout, while clean lines and a minimal visual language align the packaging with Artchur’s minimalist brand identity.

The project ultimately transforms churro tasting into a unique sensory experience, where design, culture, and gastronomy come together. By merging the ritual of eating churros with thoughtful packaging design, the set celebrates a contemporary interpretation of a distinctly Spanish experience.
