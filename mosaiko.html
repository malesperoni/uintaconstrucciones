Frozen Yougurt Branding

Mosaiko is my first university branding project, where I combined two distinct universes—Greek culture and frozen yogurt—into a single, cohesive brand identity. Inspired by traditional Greek mosaics and the iconic blue and white color palette of Greece, the brand was conceived to transport the user visually and sensorially to the Mediterranean

For the brand’s illustrations, I worked with watercolor techniques, using this medium as a distinctive visual resource. The fluidity and texture of blue watercolor were chosen to reinforce the brand’s identity, creating a recognizable aesthetic language where the presence of blue watercolors immediately evokes the world of Mosaiko.

In a contemporary context where luxury is found in craftsmanship and authenticity, Mosaiko embraces clean design and artisanal values.
