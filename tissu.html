Fashion Branding

This university project explores biomaterials, ancestral-inspired technology, and natural wool through a conceptual capsule collection. The branding draws from Patagonian gaucho culture, complemented by a curated photographic exploration.

This brand identity is inspired by the Patagonian steppes and gaucho culture. The visual language translates the landscape’s strength and austerity into a contemporary system, with a monogram reinterpreting traditional livestock branding marks as a symbol of identity and heritage.

This project explores biomaterials through the development of a vanguard outerwear piece made from sheep wool fleece, vegetable glycerin, and unflavored gelatin.

Material experimentation informed the conceptual foundation of the collection, culminating in the physical realization of the garment, complemented by accessories crafted from native Patagonian tree seeds.
